I initially created this design to emphasize functionality, but I realized it lacked visual appeal. The updated design in the carousel enhances aesthetics while maintaining usability, making it more engaging for users.

This is a partial design for smartphones which focused on a minimalistic approach, but it didn't effectively highlight key features. The revised design adds more visual hierarchy and color, making important functions stand out and improving overall user engagement.

My plan B web design is green, which made the site harder to read. The revised design which you can see in the carousel incorporates a more vibrant color palette to create a visually appealing experience that draws users in.

Using green as a background can make text and icons difficult to distinguish if the contrast isn't carefully managed. Additionally, certain shades of green might not resonate well with all users, potentially leading to a less cohesive brand experience or feelings of discomfort.
